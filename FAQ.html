A: Tyrone Stephney, who is also a songwriter, is the owner of this website.

Our goal is to bring good music back to the public, focusing on enjoyable, soothing music that carries substance and character. We particularly emphasize the creation of anti-gun violence songs and contemporary Christian music that brings comfort and draws people closer to God or their higher power.

Our music is for every man, woman, and child. We aim to connect with the youth, who often face judgment and preconceptions. We believe in giving everyone, regardless of age or background, a fair chance to achieve their goals and be treated respectfully

Our brand voice and tone on social media are centered around love and peace. We promote treating others respectfully, seeking peaceful solutions to life's problems, and challenging prejudice and bias. We encourage people to embrace the content of each person's actions and character rather than making judgments based on race, gender, or creed.
