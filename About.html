About Us

About Us

At T STEPHNEY MUSIC PRODUCTIONS-TSMC

, we are passionate about the transformative power of music. We are a music writing and production company dedicated to returning good music to the public. Our focus goes beyond mere entertainment as we strive to positively impact society by addressing important issues such as gun violence and promoting peace.

Our mission is to use music to inspire change and foster a sense of unity and understanding among people of all backgrounds. Through our heartfelt and thought-provoking songs, we aim to touch hearts, awaken minds, and spark conversations that lead to meaningful action. Led by our founder, Tyrone Stephney, a dedicated songwriter and music producer, we channel our creativity toward crafting melodies that carry substance, character, and a message.

We believe that music can heal, uplift, and connect individuals on a deep emotional level. Through this belief, we have created a collection of anti-gun violence songs and contemporary Christian music that seeks to soothe the soul and draw people back to the values of love, peace, and faith.

We understand the urgency and importance of addressing the issue of gun violence, which affects countless lives every day. Our songs aim to raise awareness, encourage dialogue, and advocate for peaceful solutions.

We believe that by amplifying the voices of those affected and creating a platform for meaningful discussions, we can contribute to a world where everyone can live without fear of gun violence. Join us on our journey to bring back good music with substance and character with TSMC!

Vision:

Our vision is to create a world where music becomes a powerful catalyst for positive change. Through our anti-gun violence songs and contemporary Christian music, we aim to inspire and uplift listeners, promoting love, peace, and the pursuit of justice

Mission:

Our mission is to bring back good music with substance, character, and a message. We are dedicated to using the power of music to raise awareness about the devastating impact of gun violence and to encourage individuals to seek peaceful solutions. Through our music writing, productions, and demo-making services, we provide a platform for artists to express their creativity while promoting social change

Our Values:

To accomplish the above Mission and Vision, We sincerely focus on the following values.

  • Resilience
  • Inclusivity
  • Positive Impact
  • Unity
  • Authenticity
  • Empathy
  • Social Justice
